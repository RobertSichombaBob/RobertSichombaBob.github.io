My Profile

Am a Cross-disciplinary professional with a BSc in Physics, specializing in AI and Machine Learning applications in software development, exploration geology, and materials characterization. Proven experience in leading data-driven and technical projects, integrating scientific and computational approaches to solve complex engineering challenges. Currently serving as Assistant Laboratory and Materials Project Manager on the 327 km Lusaka-Ndola Dual Carriageway upgrade, including the rehabilitation of 45 km of the Luanshya-Fisenge-Masangano Road across Lusaka, Central, and Copperbelt Provinces, Zambia.

My Journey

With over 3 years of experience spanning data science, geotechnical & geophysics, and materials engineering, I've developed a unique ability to bridge technical domains and deliver innovative solutions to complex challenges.
