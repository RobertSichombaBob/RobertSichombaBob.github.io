AC-16C Coarse Aggregate Characterization for High-Performance Asphalt Mixtures

Materials Engineering

Sichomba, R. (2024). AC-16C Coarse Aggregate Characterization for High-Performance Asphalt Mixtures. Journal of Materials in Civil Engineering, 36(3), 45-62.

Comprehensive technical characterization of coarse aggregate from K112 Quarry for high-performance AC-16C modified asphalt mixture in the Lusaka–Ndola Dual Carriageway project. The study evaluates aggregate properties including Los Angeles abrasion, soundness, and particle shape characteristics.
